Tiered destructibility
Every impact must produce an appropriate response
Prop tier — trash cans, benches, street lights, planters. Fully physical, Havok-simulated debris.
Structural tier — storefront glass, thin partition walls, vehicle barriers. Breakable with material-specific audio + particle.
Static tier — skyscrapers, bridges, highway pillars. Unbreakable — collision transfers full force back to vehicle.
Dynamic weather impact
2030 climate events force real gameplay adaptation
⛈

Flash flood (category 1)

Streets below 2m elevation become navigable by boat. Ground vehicles stall in 0.8m+ water depth.

⛈

Hurricane (category 2+)

Debris projectiles, reduced NPC population, helicopter no-fly zone, traction drops 40%.

☁

Heatwave

EV range reduced 18%, tire grip increased on superheated asphalt, NPCs seek shade — fewer witnesses.

Dynamic radio system
Music is a character — interruptions bridge the player and world systems
Breaking news interrupt
If player commits a high-profile crime within 90 seconds, DJ interrupts mid-song with a fictionalized report. References vehicle color, weapon type, district.
ViceGram trending segment
When Digital Footprint score exceeds 60, radio stations begin reading out player's trending posts. Increases immersion and applies pressure.
Adaptive music state
Radio switches to ambient/downbeat when police stars are cleared. Tension track plays during active chase without interrupting station selection.
Environmental audio LOD
EV engines require entirely new audio design — synthetic whine, regen braking hum, capacitor discharge on boost. Distinct identity from ICE.
